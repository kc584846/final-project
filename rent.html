"No Day But Today"

Synopsis

RENT is the 1996 Tony Award winner for Best Musical about a group of bohemian artists living in NYC, strugling with life, disease, drugs, and relationships. The musical is very powerful and very real. It is a rock show with music and lyrics by Jonathon Larson, and has seen nothing but success.

SJU

The SJU Theatre Company performed RENT the spring of 2013. It was our highest selling show in over ten years, and we could not be more proud of how the show turned out.

This was my first show at SJU, and I am beyond happy that RENT was it. The cast and crew were incredible to work with, and I knew that this is what I wante to be apart for the next three years.
